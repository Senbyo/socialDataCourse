Motivation

The dataset that was considered for this assignment tracks terror attacks across the globe. The dataset used in this project is a subset of the dataset that is found on www.kaggle.com . The subset is the data that is regarding terror attacks in Europe and this dataset was chosen as it covers terror attacks from the period 1970-2016.
