  5. Check out Figure 8 and section 4.3.

    • What is your favorite genre of narrative visualization? Why?

      Our favourite genre is the Partitioned Poster. This genre allows one to break down the information conveyed by the visualisation into multiple plots, while not necessarily requiring a strict order to exist between them. These multi-view visualizations are often miss-used in practise, though, as people tend to overload them with information and plots, ending up with a very hard to understand result.

    • What is your least favorite genre? Why?

      Our least favourite genre is the Magazine Style. One of the reasons why we do not like it is its inherent support of walls of text. Rather than having the figure complement the text, we prefer that text complements the visualizations. Another thing is that visualisations based on this genre tend to be static to keep the reader from being distracted from reading the text. That's not to say, though, that the genre itself is limited in any way to being static but rather that it is frequently under-utilized.
