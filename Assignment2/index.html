Part 2: Visualizing Murders over Time

Murders in the 5 boroughs of NYC

The figures below visualise all recorded murders in the five boroughs of New York City in the period from 2006 to 2017.
The first figure shows a timeline of all the recorded murders in the period and it is possible to filter in this timeline for a given time period.
The second figure shows how the murders are distributed in the five boroughs of New York City and it is possible to mark a part of the map to filter a selection of murders.
The third and final figure shows how the murders are distributed in the 24 hours of a day. Here it is also possible to select consecutive hours to narrow the selection even further.
