  3. Explain in your own words: What is the problem with pie-charts?

    4. We are pretty bad at estimating areas of shapes, such as areas under the curve, and a pie chart is all about comparing the size of an area with the size of another area. The same goes for angles in pie-charts. It is hard for us to correctly estimate angles ,and because this is a hard task for us, it leads to high chances of errors when analyzing a pie-chart that have parts that are roughly the same size.
